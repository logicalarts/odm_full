2. Update Procedure

To update your current version to the latest release:

  1. Rename your current opendocman folder.
  2. Unarchive opendocman and edit the config.php (Look at your old config.php file for some of the needed settings).
  3. Load the setup.php page and click on the appropriate upgrade link. (ex. http://www.mydomain.com/opendocman/setup.php)
